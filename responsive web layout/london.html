LONDON


London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a vibrant city steeped in history and culture. Famous for landmarks like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and Big Ben, it attracts millions of visitors each year. Its iconic River Thames weaves through the city, connecting historic sites with modern architectural marvels like The Shard. Known for its rich diversity, London offers a melting pot of cuisines, arts, and traditions.

As a global hub, London thrives with opportunities in finance, technology, and creative industries. The West End is a cultural hotspot, hosting world-class theaters and musicals. Lush green spaces like Hyde Park and Regent’s Park provide a peaceful escape from the bustling streets. From the British Museum to the lively markets of Camden, London caters to every kind of explorer.
